Components

The items required to build your own Pocket.

  • Raspberry Pi Zero WH - The motherboard for the Pocket.
  • Battery - A super small battery that's perfect for the Pocket.
  • Display - A vibrant and compact display.
  • Micro USB to USB A adapter - You will need this to power the display.
  • Storage - 32GB Micro SD Card for the Raspberry Pi.
  • Case - We are currently working on a case model. Keep an eye out for more updates!

Total Cost: ~$71 + shipping and tax
